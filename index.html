Crystals Collectors

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystal below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching the your total score to the random number, you lose by going over the value of the random number.

The value will reset when you press the reset buttom.
